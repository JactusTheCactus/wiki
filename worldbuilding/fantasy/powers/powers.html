The level and type of power of an individual can be denoted using the following format in Abugida:

AnB

A

  • Primary Power

n

  • Level of Primary Power

B

  • Secondary Power
Examples:
þ[6]p xi[2]xa t[1]þ xa[A]t xó[7]k
