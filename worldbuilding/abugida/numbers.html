--- layout: default title: Numbers ---
  • numbers are written ascending to the right (smallest on the left; largest on the right).
  • numbers are surrounded by []
  • digits are split into groups of three
  • groups are seperated by a space
  • example:
    • 1,000,000 = [000 000 h]
[012 345 678 9AB]
