--- layout: default title: Vowels ---
  • a vowel not prefixed by a consonant is prefixed by the null consonant: x
    • examples:
      • xa'pul.: Apple
      • xa'rm.: Arm
      • xé'r.: Ear
      • xo'd.: Odd
